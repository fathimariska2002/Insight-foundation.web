How Our Scholarships Work

A Clear, Transparent Pathway

Every scholarship follows a fully governed pathway from application through to graduation with donors kept informed at each milestone.

1

Application

A Branch Manager creates the student's profile and uploads income verification, Masjid or community recommendations, and identity documents.

2

Screening

The Foundation's Admin team reviews eligibility, confirms documentation, assesses Zakat status where applicable, and approves or requests further information.

3

Funding

The student's profile is matched to a donor whether an individual sponsor, an institutional partner, or the Endowment and Waqf Fund.

4

Disbursement

Funds are released on a fixed schedule lump sum, per-term, monthly, or milestone-based with every transaction tracked, reconciled, and auditable.

5

Reporting

Donors receive progress updates through to graduation and where the student provides consent ? into their early steps in the workforce.

This structured process ensures fairness, accountability, and full visibility for donors while safeguarding the integrity of every scholarship awarded.

Zakat & Sadaqah Stewardship

Shariah-Compliant Giving, Rigorously Managed

The Scholarship Fund maintains a strict, fully auditable separation between Zakat and Sadaqah contributions at every stage from receipt through to final disbursement.

Zakat Eligible Shariah Audited

Every Rupee Reaches Its Intended Recipient

This stewardship framework is formally endorsed by the Miskath Research Institute, Sri Lanka, ensuring that all processes adhere to recognised Shariah principles.

Donors who designate their contribution as Zakat receive a Zakat Compliance Certificate, confirming that their gift was allocated exclusively to a verified Zakat-eligible recipient and managed in accordance with Shariah-compliant guidelines.

Miskath Research Institute Endorsement

Sri Lanka's leading Shariah research body formally endorses the Foundation's Zakat stewardship processes, providing independent assurance of religious compliance and donor protection.

Zakat Compliance Certificate

Issued to every Zakat donor, confirming that your contribution was:

  • Allocated exclusively to a verified Zakat-eligible student
  • Maintained in a fully separated Zakat account
  • Disbursed according to Shariah-compliant guidelines
  • Subject to annual Shariah audit and reconciliation
  • Available for secure digital download at any time
This disciplined approach protects donor intent, safeguards religious obligations, and ensures that every contribution reaches those it is meant to uplift.
Ways to Give

Find the Right Way to Contribute

Every gift is welcome in any form, from a one-time act of Sadaqah to a permanent Named Scholarship Fund that carries your family's legacy forward.

General Scholarship Fund

Support the pool of approved, awaiting-funding students across all campuses and programmes. Your gift is matched immediately to a student in need.

Sponsor a Specific Student

Browse verified student profiles and directly fund a named student's full scholarship pathway from enrolment through to graduation.

Cohort Sponsorship

Institutional donors may sponsor a defined group such as ten students from a specific campus, programme, or community under a single agreement.

Named Scholarship Fund

Establish and name a permanent scholarship fund. All linked students are identified under that name in donor reporting a lasting legacy.

Recurring Pledges

Set a monthly or annual recurring gift and receive a reminder ahead of each due date. Sustained giving provides the most reliable support for students.

Anonymous Giving

Any donation may be made anonymously. Students simply see that they are "Supported by a Generous Donor" - your privacy is fully protected.

Our Promise of Transparency

You See Exactly Where Your Gift Goes

Personalised Impact Dashboard

Every donor - institutional or individual receives a personalised impact dashboard showing exactly which students they support, their academic progress, and with the student's consent their graduation and early employment outcomes.

Annual Tax Receipts

Tax receipts are generated automatically and made available for secure download each year ensuring donors have clear, verifiable records of how their contributions are used and the lives they help transform.

Zakat Compliance Certificates

Where applicable, Zakat Compliance Certificates are generated automatically and made available for secure download, confirming Shariah-compliant allocation and disbursement for every designated Zakat gift.
