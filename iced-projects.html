Executive Summary

A Strategic Portfolio for Educational Transformation

This portfolio presents a bold, holistic vision to transform the educational landscape in Sri Lanka. We address critical gaps across the entire ecosystem — from the student in the classroom and the teacher at the chalkboard to the principal in the office and the community that supports them.

16 Integrated Projects

Synergistic programmes designed to create a ripple effect of empowerment, innovation and sustainable development across Sri Lanka's educational ecosystem.

Self-Reinforcing Cycle

Empowered teachers inspire engaged students; strong leadership enables effective systems; a supportive community sustains progress — each project amplifies the others.

Future-Ready Nation

By investing in this comprehensive strategy, you are building the foundation for a future-ready generation and a stronger, more resilient Sri Lanka.

About ICED

Insight Centre for Education Development

ICED is an operational arm of the Insight Foundation — a limited by guarantee company registered with the Registrar of Companies.

ICED possesses the intellectual capital and proven experience — comprising educators, strategists and community leaders — necessary to drive educational impact. Its mission is to provide critical, specialised support to schools and community organisations, empowering them to drive their own development and foster adaptable projects that nurture well-rounded, future-ready citizens.

The 16 Projects

Comprehensive Project Portfolio

Each project targets a specific gap in the educational ecosystem — together they form a unified strategy for transformation.

01

Future-Ready Career Guidance Framework

Establishing structured, school-based career guidance models to bridge education and employment.

Objective

To equip every student with the knowledge and skills to make informed decisions about their future careers and higher education pathways.

Core Activities

Forming School Career Guidance Committees, grade-wise intervention programmes, industry Our Partners and career resource centres.

Impact

Confident students making strategic life choices, aligned with the demands of the 21st-century economy.

02

Public Education Awareness Campaign

Providing clear, evidence-based guidance to parents and communities on navigating modern education.

Objective

To cut through information overload and contradictory advice, empowering parents to better support their children's learning journeys.

Core Activities

Developing balanced messaging on technology, curriculum changes and timeless learning principles; leveraging credible messengers and diverse media channels.

Impact

A well-informed community that actively partners with schools for student success.

03

The Male Teacher Enrollment Project

Reversing the decline of male teachers in state schools to provide balanced role models and mentorship.

Objective

To increase male enrolment in teacher training programmes by 25% over three years.

Core Activities

Barrier analysis, targeted recruitment campaigns, scholarship advocacy and changing societal perceptions of teaching.

Impact

Improved gender balance, stronger role models for boys and enhanced school operational effectiveness.

04

The Mathematics Gap Bridge Project

A targeted intervention to tackle high failure rates in mathematics at the GCE O/L examination.

Objective

To empower students to overcome specific learning hurdles through individualised, progressive and supervised practice.

Core Activities

Development of graded-workbook series, post-classroom revision sessions with a low student-teacher ratio, and teacher/volunteer training.

Impact

Significantly improved pass rates, increased student confidence and unlocked future career pathways.

05

Holistic Primary Education Enhancement

A multi-dimensional approach to build a strong foundation for the youngest learners.

Objective

To comprehensively address teacher quality, systemic policies and learning environments in primary education.

Core Activities

Teacher pipeline development, training for untrained graduates, expert consultation for quality guidelines and creating model primary schools.

Impact

Consistent, high-quality foundational education that sets every child on a path to lifelong learning.

06

Insight Robotics & AI Programme

Immersing students in advanced technologies to foster innovation and problem-solving skills.

Objective

To develop proficiency in coding, robotics and AI through hands-on, project-based learning for Grades 8—10.

Core Activities

Bi-weekly intensive sessions, collaborative projects, competition preparation and provision of toolkits and expert trainers.

Impact

A generation of students skilled in STEM, ready for the fields of the future.

07

School Community Empowerment Programme

Strengthening the backbone of school governance for sustainable development.

Objective

To enhance the capacity and collaboration of School Management, OBAs and SDCs.

Core Activities

Training in strategic planning, financial management, project implementation and modern leadership.

Impact

Efficiently and strategically managed schools with mobilised resources and strong community Our Partners.

08

Digital Infrastructure for State Schools

A tiered strategy to bridge the digital divide and equip all schools with essential technology.

Objective

To provide tailored digital solutions based on a school's resources, from foundational computer labs to advanced 1:1 device models.

Core Activities

Infrastructure setup, teacher training on tech integration, provision of educational software and robust monitoring.

Impact

Equitable access to digital tools, enhanced learning experiences and developed digital literacy for all students.

09

Scouts & Extra-Curricular Movement

Launching and supporting scout troops and a wide array of clubs and societies.

Objective

To foster character, leadership, discipline and community service through structured co-curricular activities.

Core Activities

Weekly scout meetings, camping expeditions, community projects and establishing clubs for media, science, arts and more.

Impact

Holistic student development, improved soft skills and a strong sense of civic responsibility.

10

Teacher Empowerment & Capacity Building Retreat

"Empowered Teachers, Empowered Future" — a retreat to reinvigorate and upskill educators.

Objective

To rekindle teachers' purpose and equip them with strategies for Gen Z/Alpha learners, AI integration and work-life balance.

Core Activities

An immersive 2-day retreat with workshops on modern pedagogy, tech tools, classroom management and wellbeing.

Impact

Motivated, confident and effective teachers who create dynamic and engaging learning environments.

11

The "Healthy Teachers, Healthy Future" Initiative

Promoting NCD screening and well-being among state school teachers.

Objective

To ensure early detection and management of non-communicable diseases, safeguarding our most valuable educational asset.

Core Activities

Health awareness programmes conducted by volunteer doctors, facilitation of free screenings, counselling and distribution of well-being handbooks.

Impact

A healthier, more present and productive teaching force, positively impacting the entire school environment.

12

Prefect & Future Leaders Training Programme

Systematically cultivating the next generation of ethical and capable leaders.

Objective

To train current and potential student leaders in responsibility, governance, communication and problem-solving.

Core Activities

Leadership workshops, mentorship, project management exercises and public speaking training.

Impact

A structured leadership pipeline within schools, fostering a culture of service and accountability among students.

13

School Corporate Strategy & Annual Planning System

Instilling a culture of strategic foresight and operational excellence in school management.

Objective

To equip principals and management committees with the skills to develop and execute effective annual plans.

Core Activities

Workshops on SWOT analysis, SMART goal setting, budget alignment and monitoring & evaluation frameworks.

Impact

Schools that are proactively managed, resource-efficient and aligned with long-term development goals.

14

School Environment Model Creation

Transforming schools into beautiful, attractive and inspiring community hubs.

Objective

To create model school environments that are conducive to learning and a source of pride for the community.

Core Activities

Landscape gardening, eco-friendly initiatives (recycling, gardens), aesthetic classroom improvements and functional space redesign.

Impact

Enhanced student wellbeing, pride in the school and a tangible symbol of community value for education.

15

Senior Management Empowerment Project

Fostering a creative and innovative mindset among school principals and senior leaders.

Objective

To move beyond administrative management to transformative leadership.

Core Activities

Advanced workshops on creative problem-solving, change management, educational innovation and building a positive organisational culture.

Impact

Visionary school leaders who drive continuous improvement and inspire their staff and students.

16

Comprehensive Extra-Curricular Activity Project

A formalised framework for sports, clubs, societies and cultural competitions.

Objective

To ensure every student has access to a rich and diverse array of developmental activities outside the classroom.

Core Activities

Establishing a three-pillar system (Sports, Clubs & Societies, Competitions & Cultural Events), creating handbooks and securing institutional approval and budgets.

Impact

A vibrant school life that discovers and nurtures diverse talents — from athletics to arts and academics.
