Empowering Educators for Tomorrow's Learners

2nd September 2025 Kad/Kurukuthala Maha Vidyalaya

A teacher training program on "Empowering 21st-Century Learners: Skills for Success in a Globalized World" was successfully conducted on 2nd September 2025 for the dedicated teachers of Kad/Kurukuthala Maha Vidyalaya.

Programme Theme

The session was held under the theme: "Preparing Children to the Contemporary Changes in a Semi-Urban Context."

This initiative aimed to strengthen the capacity of teachers to equip students with the skills, mindset, and adaptability required to thrive in the ever-evolving global landscape.

Key Focus Areas

  • 21st-century learning skills
  • Adapting teaching approaches for semi-urban learners
  • Building student resilience and global awareness
  • Creative and critical thinking in the classroom

We are proud to support the teachers of Kurukuthala Maha Vidyalaya as they work to prepare their students for the challenges and opportunities of a rapidly changing world.
