Student Training Program — Good Hope College, Mawanella

29th May 2025 8:30 AM onwards Insight Technology Campus — IIMT, Mawanella

We were honored to host the vibrant students of Good Hope College Mawanella for a dynamic and engaging training session focused on personal development, leadership, and teamwork.

The students enthusiastically participated in interactive activities and learned valuable skills to support their academic and personal growth. The session was designed to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world competencies, equipping students with the mindset and tools they need to thrive.

Our Trainers

Special thanks to our esteemed trainers whose inspiring guidance and dedication made this session a success:

  • Mr. MHM Nawas
  • Mr. Ahmadh Azhar
  • Mr. Sayyaf

Key Themes Covered

  • Personal development and self-awareness
  • Leadership skills and initiative
  • Teamwork and collaborative problem-solving
  • Academic growth strategies

Kudos to all the students for their energetic participation — we look forward to seeing you thrive! This programme is part of Insight Foundation's ongoing commitment to community outreach and building meaningful partnerships with schools across the region.
