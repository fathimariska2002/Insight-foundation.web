Leadership Training for Future Trailblazers

23rd July 2025 Insight Technology Campus Al Azhar College, Hemmathagama

On July 23, 2025, Insight Technology Campus had the honour of conducting a Leadership and Values Training session for the prefects of Al Azhar College, Hemmathagama.

Facilitated by one of our passionate trainers, Mr. MHM Nawas, the session was designed to:

  • Build a balanced temperament
  • Strengthen communication skills
  • Foster team spirit through interactive engagement

Values at the Core

Rooted in values like respect, integrity, fairness, compassion, humility, accountability, resilience, perseverance, team spirit, and patriotism — the training aimed to develop young leaders with both character and competence.

It was a meaningful step toward shaping responsible and value-driven individuals who can lead with clarity and confidence.

Our heartfelt thanks to Al Azhar College for the opportunity. Together, we continue to empower the next generation!
