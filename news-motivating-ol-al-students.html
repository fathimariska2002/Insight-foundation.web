Motivating O/L & A/L Students Towards Success

2025 IIMT Campus, Mawanella

Empowering O/L and A/L students through productive and inspiring sessions conducted by Dr. N.M. Mohamed Fazal, Chief Operating Officer of IIMT Campus, focusing on contemporary topics and personal development to help students build a brighter future.

The Ambassador's visit provided a valuable opportunity to discuss potential areas of collaboration between the Sultanate of Oman and the Insight Foundation, including student exchange programmes, scholarship opportunities, and joint research initiatives.

These valuable sessions were successfully conducted at:

  • Al-Azhar Central College — Hemmathagama
  • Dippitiya Muslim Vidyalaya — Dippitiya
  • Baduriyya National School — Mawanella
  • Ganethenna Central College — Ganethenna
  • Delgahagoda Muslim Vidyalaya — Mawanella
  • Hurimaluwa Darul Uloom Vidyalaya — Rambukkana

Together, inspiring young minds and uplifting student lives for a successful future.
