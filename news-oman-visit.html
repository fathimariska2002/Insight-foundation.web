Honored Visit from the Sultanate of Oman – IIMT Campus

28th July 2025 IIMT Campus

We were privileged to host H.E. Ahmed Ali Said Al-Rashdi, the Ambassador of Oman to Sri Lanka, and Mr. Said Ali Nasser Al-Harbi, Deputy Head of Mission, at our IIMT Campus premises on Monday, 28th July.

Their visit marks a significant step in strengthening educational and diplomatic ties between our institutions.

Discussions Focused On

  • Developing our overseas campus sector in Oman
  • Creating mutually beneficial employment pathways for our students
  • Introducing Business Management programs tailored to market needs

We look forward to building impactful collaborations that empower our students and contribute to the growth of both nations.
