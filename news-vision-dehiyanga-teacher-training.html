Grassroots Education Empowerment — Teacher Training Pilot for Vision Dehiyanga

4th August 2025 Insight Institute of Management and Technology Vision Dehiyanga (VD), Kandy District

On August 4, 2025, Insight Institute of Management and Technology had the privilege of hosting a special training session for the dedicated volunteer teachers of Vision Dehiyanga (VD) — a welfare initiative based in the village of Dehiyanga, in the Kandy district.

Training Focus

Led by our passionate resource person Mr. M.H.M. Nawas, the session introduced innovative, activity-based methods for teaching English to Grade 3 students. Teachers were trained in the use of:

  • Flashcards
  • Role plays
  • Songs
  • Educational games

These methods were designed to enhance phonemic awareness, vocabulary, oral communication, reading, and writing, while also nurturing cognitive development in young learners.

Our Commitment

As part of our voluntary outreach, Insight has pledged continued support to this meaningful initiative — including:

  • Syllabus development
  • Teacher training
  • Technical assistance

This collaborative effort will serve as a pilot project, with plans to expand into other key learning areas such as the Grade 5 Scholarship Examination, depending on its future relevance.

We commend Vision Dehiyanga for their holistic approach to community education and are honored to support their mission of empowering rural learners with quality and purpose-driven learning.
