Authorised Fundraisers

Raising Funds With Trust

From time to time, the Insight Foundation authorises trusted individuals, alumni groups, or partner organisations to raise funds on its behalf for a specific campaign, scholarship fund, or project. Every Authorised Fundraiser is formally appointed in writing, given a unique reference for donor tracking, and required to remit all funds raised directly and transparently to the Foundation's official accounts.

Zakat Funds Protected

An Authorised Fundraiser is never given custody of Zakat funds outside the Foundation's own reconciliation system — ensuring complete Shariah compliance at every stage.

Full Donor Acknowledgement

Every donation raised through an Authorised Fundraiser is acknowledged directly to the donor and reconciled against the specific campaign it was raised for.

Publicly Verifiable

The public can verify any Authorised Fundraiser's status by contacting the Foundation directly before donating — protecting donors from unauthorised solicitation.

Become an Authorised Fundraiser or Verify a Fundraiser's Status

To become an Authorised Fundraiser, or to verify the status of someone claiming to raise funds on the Foundation's behalf, please contact us directly. Do not donate to anyone claiming to fundraise for the Insight Foundation without first confirming their status.
